Frequently Asked Questions

What are tribal installment loans?

Tribal installment loans are personal loans offered by lenders owned and operated by Native American tribes.

How quickly can I get approved?

Most applications receive a decision within minutes, with funds typically available within 24-48 hours of approval.

What are the requirements to apply?

Basic requirements include being 18+ years old, having a regular source of income, and a valid bank account.
