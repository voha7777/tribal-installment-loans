About TribalLoans

Our mission is to empower individuals with transparent, responsible lending options. Frequently Asked Questions

What makes tribal installment loans different?

Tribal installment loans are offered by lenders owned by Native American tribes, operating under tribal law. They often provide more flexible terms and faster approval than traditional bank loans, with structured repayment plans that make budgeting easier.

What are the eligibility requirements?

To qualify, you must be 18+ years old, have a regular source of income, maintain an active checking account, and be a U.S. resident. We consider factors beyond just credit scores for approval.

How fast can I receive my funds?

After approval, funds are typically deposited into your account within 24 hours. Applications submitted before 2 PM ET on business days often receive same-day funding.

Is there a prepayment penalty?

No, we never charge prepayment penalties. You're welcome to pay off your loan early and save on interest charges without any additional fees.

How does repayment work?

Payments are automatically deducted from your bank account on your chosen schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly). You'll receive payment reminders and can track everything through your online account.
